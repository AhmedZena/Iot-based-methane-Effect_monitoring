IOT-Based Methane
Effect Monitoring

Greenhouse gases especially methane gas CH4 has a negative impact on global warming. It is noticed that rice fields and wet lands are major sources of methane emissions. Therefore, this project presets the effect of methane produced from soil on raising the global temperature and decreasing humidity by real-time monitoring using internet of things (IOT) technology.
